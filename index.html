12 Week Year Tracker

The 12 Week Year is a system that redefines your year as 12 weeks, creating urgency and focus to achieve more in less time. By breaking down annual goals into 12-week sprints, you eliminate procrastination and maximize execution.

How to fill out this scorecard: Enter your name, list your 5 goals for the 12-week period, add up to 5 weekly actions under each goal, and mark each action as Done, Partially Done, or Not Done. Your execution score updates automatically and saves locally.
